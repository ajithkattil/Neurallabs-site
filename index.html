Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What's your typical project timeline?

Most projects range from 6-12 weeks depending on complexity. Simple POCs can be delivered in 2-4 weeks, while enterprise-scale implementations may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! All projects include 90 days of post-launch support. We also offer monthly retainer packages for continuous improvement, monitoring, and maintenance. Our support includes bug fixes, performance optimization, and feature updates.

What's your minimum project size?

We typically work on projects starting at $25K. For smaller budgets, consider our proof-of-concept packages starting at $15K, perfect for validating ideas before full implementation.

Can you work with our existing team and infrastructure?

Absolutely! We excel at collaborating with in-house teams. We can augment your staff, work as an integrated team, or handle the entire project independently. We're platform-agnostic and can work with your existing tech stack (AWS, GCP, Azure, on-premise, etc.).

How do you ensure data privacy and security?

We're SOC 2 compliant and follow industry best practices. We offer on-premise deployment, federated learning, and can sign NDAs and BAAs. Your data never leaves your infrastructure unless you explicitly choose cloud deployment. All code and models are your property.

What industries do you serve?

We work across healthcare, finance, legal tech, e-commerce, manufacturing, and technology sectors. Our AI expertise translates across industries, and we quickly adapt to domain-specific requirements.
